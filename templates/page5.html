Category Findings
Bamboo Species Selection Commercial and ornamental species (Bambusa vulgaris, Dendrocalamus asper) are highly suitable for diverse Uttarakhand climates.
Propagation Success Rhizome propagation yields 90% survival rate, ensuring rapid scaling of bamboo farming.
Training Impact Hands-on training equipped participants with skills in soil preparation, propagation, and bamboo product creation.
Economic Viability Bamboo saplings sold at INR 30-40, with mature bamboo valued higher, offering lucrative income for farmers.
Environmental Impact Bamboo absorbs high levels of CO2, contributes to sustainable resource cycles, and combats soil erosion.
Future Prospects Scalable model for other regions in Uttarakhand and nationwide. Need for better infrastructure, market linkages, and financial support for long-term success.
