Objectives of Hi-Tech Nursery Project

Key Objectives

  • PRODUCE: High-quality planting material of target bamboo species for sustainable growth.
  • STANDARDISE: Enhance and refine nursery techniques for bamboo cultivation, including the use of Mist Chambers, Hardening Chambers, and Micropropagation methods.
  • ESTABLISH: A rhizome bank for commercial bamboo species to ensure long-term availability.
  • DISSEMINATE: Share advanced bamboo propagation techniques through training and extension programs.
